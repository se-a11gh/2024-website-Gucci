                                                              MATERIAL AND CARE

                                                              Gucci products are made from carefully selected materials. Careful handling will extend the life of the product.Keep away from direct light, heat sources and rain. If the product gets wet, wipe it off immediately with a soft cloth. Store the bag in the flannel case that comes with it. To maintain its shape and absorb moisture, fill the product with thin paper.Do not carry heavy items in your bag that could cause it to deform.Wipe with a soft dry cloth.
